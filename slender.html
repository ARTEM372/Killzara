Slender: The Eight Pages Mobile

Download

Description

Android port of Slender The Eight Pages. Slender: The Eight Pages is a relatively short survival horror game played from a first-person perspective. The game takes place in a dark, dense forest at night, the player's goal is to collect eight book pages scattered throughout the forest location. The player moves slowly on foot, but is capable of running, which impairs his vision and depletes his stamina. There are no weapons or inventory in the game, the character only has a flashlight with a limited battery.

Throughout the game, the player must avoid Slenderman, a tall, pale, and faceless man who stalks and tracks the player character. The game warns of Slenderman's presence with sound cues and image static. He appears at any time and does not move when the player notices him. If the player stares at the enemy for too long, the game ends, and the only way to avoid losing is to escape and find shelter. But the more pages the character collects, the greater the chances of meeting Slenderman, and the less time is left to escape. After completing the game, the player gains additional settings, such as a day mode.
